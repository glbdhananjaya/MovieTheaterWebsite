ABOUT Nelum Kuluna Theater

Dedicated toward raising the bar in Sri Lanka’s cinema experience, Nelum Kuluna Theater and the team aims to provide our patrons with all the flare of old-school movie theaters, coupled with state-of-the-art modernity and comfort, consistent with international standards.

To be Sri Lanka’s first multi-sensory luxury entertainment company that challenges industry standards by providing creative solutions to customers from all walks of life.

  • Delivering a spectrum of diversified positive experiences to customers
  • Great Service with friendly Staff
  • Unbelievable Movie experience with modern technology
